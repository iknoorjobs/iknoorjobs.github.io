About Me

Hello, I am a PhD candidate and part-time research associate at the Natural Language Processing (NLP) group, University of Sheffield, UK, working under the supervision of Carolina Scarton and Kalina Bontcheva. My research interest lies in automated fact-checking, primarily focussing on detecting and tracking the spread of multilingual debunked narratives on digital media platforms. Please check my Curriculum vitae for more information.
